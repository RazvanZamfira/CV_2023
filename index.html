Razvan Zamfira

Information Designer at Studio Interrobang.

Our cities are inherently shaped by numerous power struggles and collaborations between the local and the global, the private and the public, the individual and society. Throughout my studies and research into architecture and urban studies I have sought to unravel this "dark matter" and to identify new tools and approaches for a 21st century model of urban governance.

Workshops and Teaching

    • A 10 hours introduction course to data visualisation for creative people. In this course you will learn how to use data as a resource to model new types of creative projects.
  • Data Visualisation in the News ROom
    • A 2 hours short introduction session on how to use new data visualisation online tools in order to improve the way journalists work with data.
